Uncanny Valley - Child Development

Kimberly A. Brink

October 23, 2017

Method

Participants

240 participants (117 females), 3 to 18 years old, were recruited.

239 remaining after removing one child with incorrect birthdate

119 children watched human-like robot

120 children watched machine-like robot

234 children watched Nao

Power

With a sample of 240 and 7 predictor variables, you should be able to detect the effect of robot on uncanny responses with a power of 1 (should be greater than .8).
